INTRODUCTION

Prime College is located in Khusibun, Nayabazar, Kathmandu. It is the first IT enabled college. The college is well equipped with excellent furnished rooms and extraordinary facilities. In addition to that, it also takes pride in its successful students spread in various fields of the world. It has been able to establish its name in the market as a producer of outstanding talented individuals. The college provides the requirements of students such as the finest directive teachers, scholars, experts and faculty members. Unlike other colleges, Prime College has facilitators that give enough space for students to work out their own plans, pursue their interests and make sure their path is eminent with their utmost guidance in every step when needed.
